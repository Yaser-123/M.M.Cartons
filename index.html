Frequently Asked Question


What types of cartons do you manufacture?

We manufacture a wide range of cartons including folding cartons, rigid boxes, and corrugated boxes to meet various packaging needs.

Can I get custom-designed cartons?

Yes, we offer custom-designed cartons tailored to your specific requirements, including size, shape, design, and printing.

What materials do you use for carton production?

We use high-quality materials such as cardboard, corrugated fiberboard, and specialty papers to ensure durability and reliability.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom cartons?

The minimum order quantity varies depending on the type and complexity of the carton. Please contact us.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Lead times depend on the order size and customization requirements. Typically, orders are fulfilled within 2-4 weeks. We will provide a more accurate timeline upon order confirmation.

Do you offer printing services for cartons?

Yes, we provide high-quality printing services, including flexographic, lithographic, and digital printing, to ensure your branding and design specifications are met.
