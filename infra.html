Infrastructure Facilities

Infrastructure Facilities In order to Overcome the ever-growing demands of our range of corrugated packaging boxes and cartons, we are equipped with a well-maintained infrastructure. Our manufacturing unit is spread over a one Acers of land Area and is installed with state-of-the-art equipment as well as manned with a team of competent workforce. We have established a vast capability for fast and effective production capacity of 100 gsm to 350 gsm of corrugate sheet.
Factory and office Area: 9000 Sq.Ft Area with Latest Industrial Wiring.
Ware House Area: 3000 Sq.Ft
Waste Store Area: 1500 Sq.Ft
Pantry/Dinning Area: 800 Sq.Ft
Staff Quarters: We have in build quarters for our Staffs with all the basic amenities.
